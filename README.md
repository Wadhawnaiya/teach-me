## 🧠 How It Actually Works (Super Simple)

You open any empty folder on your computer. That folder becomes your private classroom for one topic.

Inside it, the AI helps you create:

| What | What it does (in plain English) |
|------|---------------------------------|
| **MISSION.md** | Your personal "why" — why do you want to learn this? What will change in your life? |
| **Lessons** | Beautiful, short, interactive lessons created just for you |
| **Learning Records** | Proof of what you have actually understood (so the AI never teaches you the same thing twice) |
| **Resources** | Only the best, trustworthy materials — handpicked for you |

Every time you sit down to learn:

1. The AI looks at what you already know
2. Gives you one small, powerful lesson
3. Makes you **actively practice** (no passive watching)
4. Checks if you truly understood it
5. Plans exactly what you should learn next

It even reminds you:
- "Go take a 20-minute rest after this — your brain will remember better"
- "Sleep well tonight — this is when the magic of learning happens"
